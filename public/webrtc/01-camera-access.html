📹 WebRTC Example 1: Accessing Camera & Microphone

What this demonstrates: Using getUserMedia() to access your camera and microphone. This is the foundation of WebRTC - before you can stream to others, you need to capture media locally.
Try this:
  • Click "Start Camera" - your browser will ask for permission
  • You should see yourself on screen
  • Open the browser console (F12) to see the stream details
  • Try modifying the constraints in the code (video resolution, audio settings)
